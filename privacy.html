Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Desert Camel is a non-custodial Web3 wallet. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location.

2. Private Keys & Recovery Phrases

Your private keys and recovery phrases (seed phrases) are generated and stored locally on your device's secure storage. They are never transmitted to our servers, shared with third parties, or accessible by us. You are solely responsible for safeguarding this information.

3. Blockchain Transactions

All transactions you perform are recorded on the public SidraChain blockchain. Wallet addresses and transaction histories are publicly visible by nature. Desert Camel does not control or store this data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app connects to the SidraChain network via third-party RPC (Remote Procedure Call) providers to broadcast transactions and fetch on-chain data. These providers may collect IP addresses for network functionality but are not authorized to use your data for any other purpose.

5. Cookies & Analytics

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts that collect personal data.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect our infrastructure. However, as blockchain transactions are irreversible and your keys are locally stored, you must take precautions to prevent unauthorized access to your device.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. If you have any privacy-related concerns, you can contact us at the email below.
