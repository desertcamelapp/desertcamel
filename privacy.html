Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Desert Camel is a tap-to-earn game on SidraChain. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII).

2. Game Data & Blockchain

Your game progress, including XP, Camel XP (Cxp), and CML tokens, is recorded on the public SidraChain blockchain. This data is publicly visible by nature but not linked to your real identity.

3. Private Keys & Recovery

Your wallet private key is generated and stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or accessible by us.

4. Third-Party Services

The game connects to SidraChain via third-party RPC providers. They may collect IP addresses for functionality but are not authorized to use your data for other purposes.

5. Cookies & Analytics

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts that collect personal data.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard practices to protect our infrastructure. However, you are responsible for securing your device and private keys.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns, contact us below.
