Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I earn XP?
    Tap the camel on the main screen. Each tap gives you XP points.
  • What are Camel XP (Cxp) and CML?
    Cxp is a reward token you get by redeeming XP. CML is the utility token used to upgrade your tap power.
  • Is my wallet safe?
    Yes. Your private key is stored locally on your device. We never have access to it.
  • What is SidraChain?
    SidraChain is a fast and low-fee blockchain that powers Desert Camel.
