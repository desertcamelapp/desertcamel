Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Desert Camel is a non-custodial Web3 wallet. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, phone number, or physical location.

2. Private Keys & Recovery Phrases

Your private keys and recovery phrases are generated and stored locally on your device. They are never transmitted to our servers or accessible by us.

3. Blockchain Transactions

All transactions are recorded on the public SidraChain blockchain. Wallet addresses and transaction histories are publicly visible by nature.

4. Third-Party Services

The app connects to the SidraChain network via third-party RPC providers. These providers may collect IP addresses for functionality but are not authorized to use your data for other purposes.

5. Cookies & Analytics

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics scripts that collect personal data.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard practices to protect our infrastructure. However, you are responsible for securing your device.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. If you have concerns, contact us below.
