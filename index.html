About Desert Camel

Desert Camel is a tap-to-earn game built on the SidraChain blockchain. It combines the fun of tapping with the power of decentralized finance. Whether you're a crypto enthusiast or just looking for a fun way to earn, Desert Camel offers a unique and engaging experience.

How It Works

  • Tap: Tap the camel to earn XP points.
  • Earn: Accumulate XP and unlock achievements.
  • Redeem: Exchange XP for Camel XP (Cxp) and CML tokens.
  • Upgrade: Use CML to upgrade your tap power and earn even faster.
